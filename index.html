Welcome to the Hub

Karibu! We're Glad You're Here.

The Trans-Nzoia Community ICT Hub is a National Government initiative championed by Hon. Allan Chesang, located along KCC Road, Kitale. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable, hands-on ICT training to the people of Trans-Nzoia County.

Whether you're a student, job seeker, entrepreneur, or someone eager to build digital skills, our doors are open to you. From computer basics and internet safety to productivity tools and digital literacy, we empower individuals with practical knowledge that opens doors to education, employment, and innovation.

Join us today and take your first step toward a more connected, confident, and technology-driven future.
